wreck and reference

Wreck and Reference is an experimental rock group from Los Angeles, California, whose sound I can best describe as experimental electric anxiety.

They use a variety of aggressive synthesizers, distorted samples, and croaking vocals to deliver a sound that varies heavily from song to song- ranging from overpoweringly agressive and heavy as in Corpse Museum, to pleadingly sad in Powders- but is never pleasant and always evokes deep, intense emotion.

The first thought that may come to mind is, "Why would you listen to that, it sounds horrible!", but music can be so much more than just enjoyment. It can capture feelings that go beyond words and share them with the world. Wreck and Reference's strength as an artist doesn't lie in how enjoyable it is, but rather how profound their music is and the myriad of different emotions that they are able to capture.

That said, I do actually enjoy their abrasive sound, so that helps.
